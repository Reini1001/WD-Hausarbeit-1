Coordinate Systems and Transformation

Here we have a coordinate system of the dimensions 500 x 500 pixel. The red circle can be placed everywhere inside this coordinate system with its specific dimensions. In this example it starts at the point (420,70) and moves the coordinates (70,420).

The green rectangles can be manipulated by hovering over them. The rectangle in the upper left corner is skewed in an angle of 20 degrees. In the lower right corner the rectangle is scaled by 1.1.
